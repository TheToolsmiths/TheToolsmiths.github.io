<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-theme="dark"><p dir="ltr" lang="en">In the action, store also the index of which object, along with any other information needed to tell exactly what it is that the action represents.<br/><br/>The action has two virtual method for undo and redo. Sometimes the implementation of the two is identical...<br/><br/>2/n</p>‚Äî Max Liani (@maxliani) <a href="https://twitter.com/maxliani/status/1395008968163045377?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw">May 19, 2021</a></blockquote>

<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-theme="dark"><p dir="ltr" lang="en">Everything should be a value change.
